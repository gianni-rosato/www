Hi, I'm Gianni. I build video & image compression technology for a more beautiful Internet.

Some of what I have worked on includes:

  • Iris-WebP, the world's best WebP encoder
  • Eve-AV1 & Eve-VP9, commercial video encoders developed by Two Orioles
  • SVT-AV1-PSY, a fork of the SVT-AV1 encoder with a focus on perceptual quality
  • The Codec Wiki, a public resource for digital compression knowledge

You can find more on my GitHub or the Projects page. I also enjoy contributing to open-source projects, including SVT-AV1.
